## 👋 About Us

**China Mobile (Hong Kong) Innovation Research Institute Co., Limited** is an international innovation and research platform established to connect global R&D resources, strengthen cross-border collaboration, and support the global expansion of China Mobile’s core capabilities and products.

Based in Hong Kong and facing the world, we aim to leverage Hong Kong’s unique role as an international bridge to build a globally collaborative R&D ecosystem. We focus on high-quality international technology cooperation, global talent engagement, industry-academia-research collaboration, and international organization operations.

Our mission is to support Hong Kong’s development as an international innovation and technology center while contributing to the digital and intelligent transformation of the global information and communications industry.

---

## 🚀 Strategic Focus Areas

### Artificial Intelligence

We explore large model applications, frontier AI research, and practical AI solutions that can be integrated into real-world industry scenarios.

### Web 3.0

We conduct research on Web 3.0 technologies, applications, and emerging industrial ecosystems, with a focus on trusted digital infrastructure and next-generation digital services.

### 6G

We advance research in 6G frontier technologies, international standards, intelligent network integration, and scenario-based validation for future mobile information systems.

---

## 🌐 What We Do

### Global Expansion of Technology Capabilities

We support the international adaptation and innovation of domestic products and technical capabilities, especially for markets across the Belt and Road region and other global markets.

### International Research Collaboration and Talent Engagement

We work with universities, research institutions, enterprises, and industry partners in frontier fields such as AI, Web 3.0, and 6G to promote breakthrough innovation and attract world-class research talent.

### International Organization Operations

We support the operation of international industry cooperation platforms, including GTI-related initiatives, to strengthen collaboration across Asia-Pacific, Belt and Road markets, and the broader global information and communications ecosystem.

---

## 🧭 Our Long-Term Vision

We are committed to becoming a leading international new-type research institution between 2026 and 2030.

Our development roadmap includes:

* **2024:** Establishing our foundation in Hong Kong and integrating into the global innovation network.
* **2025:** Building institutional mechanisms, improving governance, and developing international influence in science and technology innovation.
* **2026–2030:** Becoming a leading international research organization with global collaboration capabilities and sustainable innovation impact.
